HM-0710

Control Panel 2, with Back-Up Battery

Control Units, RRS All Products

Description

  • Product Specification:Incl. full set of cable glands and Ground Connection Device
  • Backup Battery:Up to 20 h, rechargable
  • Dimensions:125x122x83,5 mm
  • Inputs:2 + 4
  • Outputs:10 + 2
  • Weight:2.62kg
Notes:
The Control Panel 2 is a Control Unit in an Electronic Remote Release System; it is Hammar's most advanced Control Unit, a customised solution for activation of multiple lifesaving appliances, evacuation systems or loads. The Unit is capable of handling up to 10 Release Units (ERU) and 2 Input Sensors, and another 2 ERUs + 4 Input Sensors with small modifications. A Control Panel 2 can play a centralised role in a controlled, safe and fast evacuation of a vessel. CP2 is programmed on delivery based on the setup on the individual vessel and can activate ERUs individually or in a predefined sequence differentiated in time. It is a self-checking system and will not bring attention until awoken from its idle state or an error is detected. The CP2 may be used to open hatches or close fire doors. The HM-0710 has a rechargeable back-up battery (HM-0725) in case of power failure on the ship's 24V DC emergency power supply.
