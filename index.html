H20 Release Units

The Hammar Hydrostatic Release Units (HRU) are designed to be safe and reliable in extreme conditions. The H20 is the best-selling HRU in the world and has been saving lives at sea for more than 40 years.

Systems for Remote Release

The Hammar Remote Release Systems (RRS) enable you to release liferafts, lifebuoys, or evacuation systems from the bridge or other strategic locations onboard. The systems offer flexible solutions with many distinct advantages in terms of safety and cost.

Lifejacket Inflators

The Hammar Lifejacket Inflators are developed and tested during the roughest conditions. Worn by professionals worldwide, they are small, smart, and trustworthy devices that provide full assistance when you need it.
