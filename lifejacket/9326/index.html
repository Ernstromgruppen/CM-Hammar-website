URSUIT DSH 275N

  • bouyancy275

Where to buy

Ursuit DSH Hydro 275N is a lightweight, comfortable automatic lifevest with an actual buoyancy of 320N.

The lifevest has a Quick Burst ™ YKK zipper with a closed pattern (designed especially for Lifevests), and reflectors that go around the whole lifevest.

User comfort is enhanced with a neoprene collar mesh triangle which prevents air buildup under the clothes.

The lifevest has an adjustable waistband, crotch strap, marine-grade whistle and oral inflation tube.

The maintenance interval is 5 years. An inspection window indicates that the inflator mechanism and the cartridge are in working order.

The metallic buckles on the vest are anti-magnetic and have a D-ring for (ISO 12401) attaching the safety rope.

CE, ISO 12401, Coastal and offshore. The weight of the vest is 1100 grams and the warranty is 3 years
